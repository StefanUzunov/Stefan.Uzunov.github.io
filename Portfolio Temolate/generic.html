Snakes World

This game was made in Game Editor.

It contains one level, enemies and collectibles which must be obtained in order to unlock the exit. This was a project I was assigned during sixth form and had to create a working game. I used different terrains for different areas and made the enemies so they constantly follow the character instead of moving left and right until they hit a wall.
